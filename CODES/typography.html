Typography

Concept Note: For my Futura typography poster, I focused on its use in the Apollo 11 mission,
where astronauts left a plaque on the moon written in Futura. I made the plaque the centerpiece with a
zoomed-in image. Inspired by El Lissitzky's style, I included key features of the font from my research.
It was a fun and creative project!

Apps used: Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
Time taken: 2 weeks
