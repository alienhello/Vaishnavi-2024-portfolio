Brochure

Concept Note: Created a brochure for Koolar and Co, an Irani café in Matunga,
showcasing its popular bun muska, tea, and cookies. It highlights the café's history,
its impact on popularizing Irani cafés in India, and films shot there. The brochure uses
a retro red plaid and white design to reflect the classic café atmosphere.

Apps used: Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
Time taken: 1 Week
